How to organize and analyze research data

For this lesson, you'll need the following:

  1. Microsoft Excel
  2. PASW (formerly SPSS) statistics software, or free trial download
You should already be familiar with:
  1. The basic functions and tools of Excel
  2. Descriptive statistics and inferential statistics
  3. Research design and how to identify independent variables and dependent variables

This short lesson is intended for graduate and advanced undergraduate students undertaking original research projects. After completing the 30-minute lesson, you will be able to to:

  1. Format raw research data in an Excel spreadsheet for efficient analysis
  2. Run basic descriptive statistics in Excel
  3. Prepare the document for statistical analysis in the software package PASW
